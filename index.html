ACCOMMODATIONS

ON-SITE

Gilbertsville Farmhouse has beautiful places to stay – from private A-frame cabins to elevated glamping tents. These are first come, first served via your RSVP. There are more guests than on-site lodging.

Each site has a king-sized bed that sleeps two. Stay with a friend, loved one, or solo. The more friends who double up the merrier. (It’s possible to bring a blow-up mattress.) Lodging is $500 per glamping site, which covers 2 nights. Note that one-night stays on-site aren’t possible, but there are off-site options below.

OFF-SITE
